About us

Coco’s Chocolatiers, is a home business born in 2016 in Mexico, where variety of cocoa species grow, and one of the major sources of cocoa of the best chocolate companies in the world. The founder is a swiss chef named Lebreton Bassanin, who has a degree in chocolatery and started his own business of organic chocolate products, which is our speciality among a variety of many other candy we offer on our stores. we have grown very fast with the help of many agronomists from the island who work every day to make our products have the best propieties for you.

On this webpage you can chose from our list of organic ingredients and different flavors to create your own chocolate product, then we will make it and ship to where ever you want. we do all artesanally way. besides, you can chose an make an order from all of the other candy products that coco's store offer to our clients.
